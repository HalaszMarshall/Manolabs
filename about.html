Who we are
We are a one-stop hardware and software development company dedicated to building solutions with the newest technology.
We do this by providing multiple services: consulting, hardware development, embedded software development, web development, quality assurance and maintenance.
From the idea through consultance we design and build the whole system including devices and interface.
We consider ourselves open-minded, passionate and always eager to explore new technology.
We started in 2020 with a team of 2 people and are growing steadily.
Our clients are mostly from US and Europe.
We have projects in the fintech, medical and entertainment industries.
What we do
We build circuits of all kinds, starting from small ones like a key chain to larger ones like LED boards.
The circuits are also programmed within our labs by our embedded software team.
We also design and build systems around them, be it on desktop or the web.
Our main drive is passion for technology which is reflected through our personal and business projects.
